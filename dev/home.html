I Ching - Wisdom From The East

Think of a question in your mind. Then press the button to see one of the Hexagrams from the I Ching. Hexawhats now? The I Ching (or Book of Changes, originated in Asia, thousands of years old) is composed of 64 Hexagrams, each representing a facet of deep knowledge. Why 64? You flip a coin, if it's heads you draw a straight line, if it's tails you draw a broken line. Do it 6 times and you end up with a Hexagram. Traditionally, the Seeker (you) will hold a question or state in their mind. Their mind and heart are to remain open, to receive the Wisdom from the soul or the subconscious or whatever. So go ahead, press the button when you're ready and apply what you read to your experience and path.
